Solving the College Admissions Puzzle

The college search and application process can be overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be. At College Connectors, we’re seasoned college planning consultants with years of experience helping students and their families navigate the process with ease.

Let's Get Started!

The Student

Just like every college, every student is unique. We focus on the particular needs of each student by identifying their values, dreams, and even roadblocks.

LEARN MORE »

The College

We spend time educating students on each potential college’s characteristics, such as location, diversity, graduation rates, and even scholarship policies.

LEARN MORE »

The Fit

Once the student understands more about themselves and the colleges, we help narrow down the list and assist with the application processes of each one.

LEARN MORE »

The Decision

We guide students and families decide between multiple acceptance letters, examining the costs, scholarships, programs, and environments of each college.

LEARN MORE »

COLLEGE PLANNING

Moving toward independence is an exciting time in any student’s life. But thinking about their future and trying to make an educated decision about college can be intimidating. We want to help students and families feel prepared for the college decision long before the application deadlines arise.
